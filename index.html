Peter Parker a.k.a Spider-Man

Your Friendly Neighbourhood Spider-Man


Spider-Man, the webslinger who fights for justice.


Here's a timeline of Spider-Man's life:


  • 2001 - Born in Queens,New York City on August 10, as Peter Benjamin Parker.
  • 2010 - Visited the Stark Expo and acquired toy replicas of the Iron Man helmet and glove, as well as Tony Stark's autograph.
  • 2015 - Bitten by a radioactive spider and acquires superhuman powers.
  • 2016 - Attended Midtown School of Science and Technology.
  • 2017 - Defeated the Vulture, the alien weapons dealer Adrian Toomes.
  • 2018 - Joins Nick Fury in defeating supervillain Mysterio, who uses special effects to commit crimes.
  • 2019 - Dies as a result of 'the snap' during the Avenger's battle with Thanos.
  • 2023 - Ressurected when Thanos was defeated by the Avengers.
  • 2024 - Marries his highschool sweetheart and romantic partner, Mary Jane Watson. The two lived happily ever after.

Trivia:


  • Spider-Man does not wear underwear beneath his suit.
  • Peter's spidey-sense helps him know when to flip pancakes to get them perfect.
  • Peter's aunt, May, is a bombshell.
